Momo

Description

Momo is a Nepali dumpling famous in Nepal and Tibet along with neighouring countries. Momo can come in different shapes and with differnt flavour of stuffing inside.

Ingredients

  • "Kima"
  • Chopped onoions
  • salt
  • coriendar
  • Pitho

Steps

  1. Make circular pitho.
  2. Add "kima" in pitho.
  3. Transform the pitho into the shape of desired Momo.
  4. Add momo into the momo utensil.
  5. Steam the momo for 20- 25 minutes.
