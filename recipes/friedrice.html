Egg Fried Rice

Description

Egg fried rice is a popular meal around the world, especially in Asia and Europe. It is an easy to make dish which can be cooked in under 10 mintues.

Ingredients

  • cooked rice
  • eggs
  • chopped onions
  • oil or ghee butter

Steps

  1. Pour ghee or oil of your choice in frying pan.
  2. Make scrambeled eggs with onions.
  3. Remove the eggs and then fry the rice for 5 minutes
  4. Add the cooked eggs and stir them together for 1 mintues.
