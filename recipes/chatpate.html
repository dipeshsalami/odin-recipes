Chatpate

Chatpate is a famous street dish from India. It is popular in South and South-East Asian countires like Nepal, Bhutan. The dish contains raw noodles mixed with boiled potatoes, salad and "bhuja

Ingredients

  • Noodles (preferably wai:wai)
  • Boiled potatoes
  • "Bhuja"
  • Boiled beans
  • Salads

Steps

  1. Add bhuja in a container.
  2. Chop the boiled potato and add it in with bhuja.
  3. Add noodles and the flavouring contained in it.
  4. Add any other spices you want for certain flavouring.
  5. Add a tea spoon sunflower oil.
  6. Add salads.
  7. Stir till all the ingredients are completely mixed.
